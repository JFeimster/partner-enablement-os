# Partner Enablement OS

Partner Enablement OS is the source-of-truth repository for Moonshine Capital partner onboarding, launch kits, training assets, referral partner resources, outreach scripts, activity trackers, prompt chains, and publishing workflows.

This repo is intentionally broader than affiliate launch kits. It supports affiliates, funding partners, referral partners, operators, local connectors, and future channel programs.

## Core Purpose

- Store reusable partner enablement content.
- Keep launch kits and partner-specific assets version-controlled.
- Provide source material for Notion, Wix, HubSpot, Gmail, Google Drive, NotebookLM, Flash-UI, and automation workflows.
- Reduce duplicate content across partner onboarding systems.

## Primary Sections

- `launch-kits/` — partner-specific launch kits and field assets.
- `onboarding/` — reusable onboarding sequences and first-step resources.
- `training/` — core partner training and operating playbooks.
- `referral-partner-assets/` — audience-specific handouts and scripts.
- `scripts/` — universal outreach, follow-up, objection handling, and intake copy.
- `trackers/` — partner activity, pipeline, and referral tracking templates.
- `systems/` — integration notes for Notion, HubSpot, Wix, Gmail, Google Drive, Tally, and NotebookLM.
- `prompts/` — prompt libraries for ChatGPT, NotebookLM, Flash-UI, Notion AI, and email sequences.
- `templates/` — reusable launch kit and asset templates.
- `archive/` — deprecated drafts, experiments, and superseded material.

## Naming Rule

Use `partner` as the ecosystem umbrella unless a file specifically refers to a role, such as bank manager, accountant, attorney, broker, affiliate, or referral source.
