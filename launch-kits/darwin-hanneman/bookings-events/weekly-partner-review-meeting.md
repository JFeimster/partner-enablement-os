## Booking Service Name

Weekly Funding Partner Review

## Internal Purpose

Use this recurring meeting to keep Darwin focused on relationship-building activity, referral partner conversations, submitted applications, follow-ups, and next-week priorities.

This is not a motivational hangout. It is a field review.

---

## Public / Calendar Description

A weekly partner review session to review recent referral conversations, business owner conversations, funding applications, follow-ups, lessons learned, and next-week priorities.

The goal is simple: keep the pipeline moving, identify stuck points, and focus the next week on high-value conversations.

---

## Best-Fit Attendees

- Darwin Hanneman
- Moonshine Capital operator / partner manager
- Optional future team members or support staff

---

## Meeting Agenda

1. Review last week’s activity
2. Review referral partner conversations
3. Review business owner conversations
4. Review applications submitted
5. Review follow-ups completed
6. Identify stuck deals or unclear next steps
7. Capture lessons learned
8. Set next week’s priorities
9. Confirm daily activity targets

---

## Weekly Review Questions

- How many referral partner conversations happened last week?
- How many business owner conversations happened last week?
- How many applications were started or submitted?
- Which audience produced the best conversations?
- Which conversations need follow-up this week?
- What objection or confusion showed up more than once?
- Which asset needs improvement?
- What is the highest-value target for next week?
- What is one action Darwin should stop doing?
- What is one action Darwin should double down on?
