Clone This Structure

Future Partner kits should follow the Darwin pattern.

  1. Partner profile. Define the Partner lane and positioning.
  2. Strongest lanes. Identify referral audiences and vertical opportunities.
  3. Available assets. Link source files, Drive exports, trackers, scripts, and handouts.
  4. Launch rhythm. Include a practical 30-day plan without outcome guarantees.
  5. Safe language. Include what to say and what not to say.
  6. Source paths. Keep GitHub paths and Drive IDs visible in the data layer.

Compliance Reminder

Launch kits should not guarantee applications, approvals, funding, rates, terms, amounts, timelines, revenue, income, qualification, or outcomes.
