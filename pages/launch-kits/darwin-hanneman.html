Darwin Hanneman Launch Kit

Darwin Hanneman is a relationship-first commercial funding Partner backed by Moonshine Capital. His launch strategy focuses on referral partners who already know business owners with funding problems.

Darwin helps business owners explore funding options that may be available based on business profile and provider criteria. Approval, terms, amounts, and timing are not guaranteed.

Best-Fit Referral Lanes

Darwin should fish where trust already exists.

Darwin’s strongest launch lanes are referral sources who already hear business owners talk about timing pressure, delayed financing, equipment needs, payroll pressure, materials, repairs, growth, or working capital.

Bank Managers

Responsible next step when traditional financing is delayed, unavailable, or not the right fit today.

Accountants

Cash-flow timing, tax-season pressure, receivables, payroll, and capital planning conversations.

Attorneys

Business clients during transactions, disputes, expansions, and operational pressure.

Equipment Dealers

Equipment purchases, repairs, replacement, upgrades, and operating capital needs.

Trucking Businesses

Fuel, repairs, insurance, equipment, payroll, and receivables timing gaps.

Construction Businesses

Project timing, materials, labor, mobilization, retainage, and seasonal pressure.
