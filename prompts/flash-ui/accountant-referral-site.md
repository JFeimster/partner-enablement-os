## Prompt

Create a high-converting static landing page for a commercial funding referral program targeting CPAs, bookkeepers, tax professionals, and small business financial advisors.

Brand: Moonshine Capital  
Page name: Funding Resource for Accountants  
Audience: accountants and advisors whose clients need working capital, tax cash-flow support, equipment funds, or growth capital.  
Goal: Get accountants to refer business clients to a trusted commercial funding resource.

Style:
Clean professional fintech with editorial trust. White background, navy typography, muted gold accent, polished cards, calculator/dashboard-inspired visuals. Should feel credible enough for CPAs but not painfully corporate.

Core message:
Your clients already ask you money questions. Give them a practical funding resource without becoming their lender.

Sections:

1. Hero headline:
   “When Your Client Needs Capital, Give Them a Smarter Next Step.”
2. Subheadline:
   “Moonshine Capital helps business owners explore funding options through one simple application and multiple potential funding paths.”
3. CTA buttons:
   “Refer a Client”
   “Schedule a Partner Intro”
4. Pain points:
   - tax bills
   - payroll gaps
   - expansion
   - inventory
   - equipment
   - slow receivables
   - seasonal cash flow
5. Advisor benefit:
   Help clients without taking on underwriting, lending, or sales pressure.
6. Who to refer:
   - growing businesses
   - cash-flow stressed businesses
   - owners denied by banks
   - businesses needing fast working capital
   - companies preparing for expansion
7. How it works:
   Step 1: Share link or make intro.
   Step 2: Client completes short application.
   Step 3: Funding options are reviewed.
   Step 4: Client receives next-step guidance.
8. Trust section:
   Relationship-first. Clear expectations. No guaranteed approval claims.
9. Referral handout download CTA.
10. QR-code placeholder.
11. Compliance footer.
