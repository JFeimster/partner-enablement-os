---

Create a high-converting static landing page for a commercial funding referral program targeting bank managers, branch managers, and business bankers.

Brand: Moonshine Capital  
Page name: Bank Decline Referral Partner Program  
Audience: bankers who regularly decline business owners but still want to preserve the client relationship.  
Goal: Get bank managers to refer business owners who do not qualify for traditional bank financing.

Style:
Professional fintech, clean, credible, dark navy and white, subtle gold accent, trustworthy but not boring. No crypto vibes. No hype-bro nonsense. Should feel like a serious alternative finance partner.

Core message:
When your bank has to say no, Moonshine Capital may still help your business client explore realistic funding options through one simple application and multiple funding paths.

Sections:

1. Hero section with bold headline:
   “When the Bank Has to Say No, Give the Business Owner a Next Step.”
2. Subheadline:
   “Refer clients who don’t fit traditional bank criteria to a commercial funding partner built to help them explore alternative options.”
3. CTA buttons:
   “Refer a Business Owner”
   “Book a Partner Call”
4. Problem section:
   Banks decline good business owners every week because of credit, time in business, cash flow, collateral, documentation, or underwriting limits.
5. Solution section:
   One application. Multiple funding paths. Relationship-first follow-up.
6. Who to refer:
   - declined business loan applicants
   - business owners needing working capital
   - equipment or expansion needs
   - short time in business
   - cash-flow gaps
   - not enough collateral
7. How it works:
   Step 1: You introduce or share the referral link.
   Step 2: The client completes a short application.
   Step 3: We review possible funding paths.
   Step 4: The client gets next-step guidance.
8. Why bankers like this:
   - preserve client trust
   - offer a helpful next step
   - avoid sending clients into random online loan chaos
   - no pressure, no weird pitch
9. Referral CTA block with QR-code placeholder.
10. Compliance footer:
   “Funding is not guaranteed. Approval, terms, amounts, and timing depend on applicant qualifications and provider requirements.”
