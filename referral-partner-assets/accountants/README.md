# Accountant Referral Assets

Canonical source assets for accountants, CPAs, bookkeepers, tax professionals, and small business financial advisors.

## Audience

Professionals who see client cash-flow pressure early through books, payroll, taxes, receivables, inventory, growth costs, and operating expenses.

## Core Pain Points

- Clients may be profitable on paper but tight on cash.
- Tax bills, payroll, vendors, receivables, and growth costs can create urgent capital needs.
- Advisors want to help without becoming lenders or stepping outside their professional lane.
- A funding referral can give clients a practical next step while preserving the advisor relationship.

## Canonical Assets

Recommended source files:

- `one-pager.md`
- `printable-handout.md`
- `outreach-script.md`
- `follow-up-script.md`
- `landing-page-copy.md`

## Partner Variant Strategy

Use these canonical assets as the source. Create a partner-specific version under `launch-kits/{partner-name}/` only when adding partner contact info, QR/application link, local market notes, partner-specific lanes, or launch-specific positioning.
