# Attorney Referral Assets

Canonical source assets for small business attorneys, business formation attorneys, contract attorneys, acquisition attorneys, and startup/legal advisors.

## Audience

Attorneys and legal professionals who work with business owners during formation, expansion, contracts, acquisitions, restructuring, disputes, and growth decisions where capital may become the next obstacle.

## Core Pain Points

- A legal transaction or business plan may stall because capital is missing.
- Clients may need launch capital, acquisition support, equipment funds, working capital, or project funding.
- Attorneys need a responsible funding resource without providing financial advice or becoming the lender.
- A clean referral can help the client explore options while the attorney stays in their professional lane.

## Canonical Assets

Recommended source files:

- `one-pager.md`
- `printable-handout.md`
- `outreach-script.md`
- `follow-up-script.md`
- `landing-page-copy.md`

## Partner Variant Strategy

Use these canonical assets as the source. Create a partner-specific version under `launch-kits/{partner-name}/` only when adding partner contact info, QR/application link, local market notes, partner-specific lanes, or launch-specific positioning.
