## Short DM Script

```text
Hey [First Name] — quick question.

When your business clients need a capital option that does not fit your usual lane, do you already have a reliable next-step resource?

I help business owners explore possible options through Moonshine Capital. No pressure — just a responsible referral path when it makes sense.
```

## Email Script

```text
Subject: Quick referral resource for business clients

Hey [First Name],

I wanted to introduce myself because I help business owners explore possible capital options when they need a responsible next step.

If you ever have a client who needs to review available options, I would be glad to be a resource.

Approval, terms, amounts, and timing are not guaranteed, but one intake can help identify possible paths based on the business profile.

Open to a quick intro call sometime this week?

[Partner Name]
```

## Phone Opener

```text
Hey [First Name], this is [Partner Name]. I will be quick.

I work with Moonshine Capital helping business owners explore possible capital options. I am reaching out because you likely know business owners who occasionally need a responsible next step when the usual path is not a fit.
```

## Follow-Up 1

```text

```

## Follow-Up 2

```text

```

## Referral Ask

```text
If someone comes to mind who needs to explore options, send them my way and I will make sure the conversation is handled professionally.
```
