## Purpose

Use this tracker to measure weekly partner activity, referral partner conversations, business owner conversations, funding applications, follow-ups, lessons learned, and next-week priorities.

This is the canonical source tracker. Partner-specific versions should only be created when targets, audiences, or launch priorities are materially customized.
